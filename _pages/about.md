I am currently a Member of Research Staff at [The Voleon Group](https://voleon.com/), where I develop and study machine learning models for applications in financial markets. I received my Ph.D. in Econometrics and Statistics from the Booth School of Business, University of Chicago, in 2025. I was fortunate to be advised by [Prof. Mladen Kolar](https://mkolar.coffeejunkies.org/) and [Prof. Cong Ma](https://congma1028.github.io/).

## Research Interests

My research broadly spans data science, with a particular focus on the intersection of statistics, optimization, and machine learning. I am interested in developing novel methodologies that are both theoretically grounded and practically impactful. My current research interests include:

- Distribution Shift (e.g., Transfer Learning)
- Probabilistic Graphical Models
- Distributed and Federated Learning
- Data Markets and Data Valuation

I am always open to exploring new research directions and collaborations. If you are interested in working together, please feel free to reach out via email.
