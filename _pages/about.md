I am currently a Member of Research Staff at [The Voleon Group](https://voleon.com/). Previously, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Econometrics and Statistics from the Booth School of Business, University of Chicago, advised by [Prof. Mladen Kolar](https://mkolar.coffeejunkies.org/) and [Prof. Cong Ma](https://congma1028.github.io/).

## Research Interests

My research often lies in the intersection of statistics, optimization, and machine learning, with a focus on developing novel methodologies with both practical applications and theoretical guarantees. Specific areas include:

- Distribution Shift (e.g., Transfer Learning)
- Probabilistic Graphical Models
- Distributed/Federated Learning
- Data Market and Data Valuation
